About Melissa Scott

Melissa Scott grew up in Salt Lake City, Utah before moving to North Carolina in middle school. She enjoys the outdoors, travel, and being with her family. Melissa is passionate about helping others to get to know cultures and peoples from across the world. She hopes that her photography gives people a small glimpse into other cultures and places.
